Before the Ash

A haunting journey through a world on the brink of destruction.

Game Overview

Before the Ash is an atmospheric adventure game that takes players on a journey through a world teetering on the edge of apocalypse. As one of the last survivors, you must navigate through haunting landscapes, solve intricate puzzles, and uncover the truth about what led to this devastation.

With its stunning visuals and immersive sound design, Before the Ash creates an unforgettable gaming experience that will stay with you long after you've put down the controller.

Game Features

  • Explore a beautifully rendered post-apocalyptic world
  • Engage with challenging puzzles that test your ingenuity
  • Experience a deeply emotional narrative that unfolds as you progress
  • Interact with the few remaining survivors and discover their stories
  • Make choices that impact the outcome of your journey

How to Play

Before the Ash can be played directly in your browser. Use the following controls:

Keyboard Controls:

  • W, A, S, D or Arrow Keys: Movement
  • Space: Jump / Interact
  • E: Examine Objects
  • Tab: Inventory
  • Esc: Pause Menu

Mobile Controls:

  • On-screen joystick: Movement
  • Action buttons appear contextually
  • Two-finger tap: Pause Menu

The Story Behind the Game

In a world ravaged by environmental catastrophe, Before the Ash follows the journey of a lone survivor seeking answers and possibly, redemption. As ash falls from the sky and resources dwindle, you must navigate through the remnants of civilization, piecing together what happened and discovering if there's any hope for the future.

The game draws inspiration from classic post-apocalyptic fiction while offering a unique perspective on themes of loss, hope, and human resilience in the face of overwhelming odds.
